OntOlogy Pitfall Scanner!

OOPS! (OntOlogy Pitfall Scanner!) helps you to detect some of the most common pitfalls appearing when developing ontologies.

To try it, go to OOPS! homepage. and enter a URI or paste an OWL document. A list of pitfalls and the elements of your ontology where they appear will be displayed.
